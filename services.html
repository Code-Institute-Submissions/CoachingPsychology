Executive Coaching

One-to-one coaching

Programmes are typically 6-12 months which cover Executive Coaching for individuals to help manage personal changes (e.g. confidence, impact, influencing, conflict) or a specific role or situation (e.g. on-boarding, business growth, return from maternity, succession planning, feeling overwhelmed with work)

Coaching Clinics

As above but the coach can work with up to 4 individuals in pre-booked slots across a day. Coachees typically have 1-3 sessions.
